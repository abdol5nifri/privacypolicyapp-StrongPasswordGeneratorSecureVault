Privacy Policy

Strong Password Generator – Secure Vault respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user data.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app works completely offline and does not require account creation, login, or internet access.

2. Stored Data

All generated passwords and saved notes are stored locally on your device only using internal storage.

  • Passwords are never uploaded to any server
  • Data is never shared with third parties
  • Data remains fully under your control

You can delete all saved passwords at any time from within the app.

3. Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive permissions.

Clipboard access is used only when the user chooses to copy a generated or saved password.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party SDKs
  • Tracking tools

5. Data Security

To protect your saved passwords, the app provides:

  • Local storage only
  • Optional Master PIN protection
  • Temporary password reveal with auto-hide

6. Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be reflected within the app or its store listing.
