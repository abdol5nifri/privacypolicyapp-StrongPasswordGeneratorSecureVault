Privacy Policy

Strong Password Generator
Developer: Morad Laidi

1. Information Collection

Strong Password Generator does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works completely offline and does not require account creation, login, or internet access.

2. Stored Data

All generated passwords and saved notes are stored locally on your device using internal storage only.

  • Passwords are never uploaded to any server
  • Data is never shared with third parties
  • You can delete all saved data at any time within the app

3. Permissions

The app does not request sensitive permissions. Clipboard access is used only when the user chooses to copy a password.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or third-party SDKs.

5. Data Security

The app provides local-only storage, optional Master PIN protection, and temporary password reveal with auto-hide.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be reflected within the app or its store listing.
