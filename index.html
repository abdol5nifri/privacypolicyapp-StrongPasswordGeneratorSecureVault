Privacy Policy

App Name: Strong Password Generator
Developer: Morad Laidi

Strong Password Generator respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works completely offline and does not require account creation, login, or internet access.

2. Stored Data

  • All generated passwords, saved notes, and security data are stored locally on your device only
  • Passwords are never uploaded to any server
  • Data is never shared with third parties
  • Data remains fully under your control
  • You can delete all saved passwords at any time from within the app

3. Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive permissions. Clipboard access is used only when the user explicitly chooses to copy a password.

4. Third-Party Services

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No third-party SDKs
  • No tracking tools

5. Data Security

  • Local storage only
  • Optional Master PIN protection
  • Temporary password reveal with automatic hiding

6. Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if necessary. Any changes will be reflected within the app or its store listing.
